Documenting all things electrical

Onboarding continues to be a challenge in this odd sections of the integrator industry, where the old analog world clashes with the new digital age. This site is dedicated to sharing knowledge and expertise around electrical power monitoring, and aims to ease the pain for all road warriors alike.

Why Manatee Bros

Why manatees? They are known for their gentle and peaceful nature, and are suspiciously close to the word "monitor" when you've had your two corporate sponsored entertainment drinks for the night. After a night out slurring "I design maniteering systems", the Manatee Bros were born.
